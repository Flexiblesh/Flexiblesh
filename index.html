OUR VALUES AND COMMITMENTS

  • Solidarity

    Flexibles Helps's work is based on its responsibility to help the most disadvantaged and to support them in improving their daily lives.

  • Impartiality

    Flexibles Helps rejects all forms of discrimination in its actions and focuses solely on helping the most vulnerable.

  • Humanity

    Flexibles Helps is committed to fighting human and animal suffering, which it considers intolerable, and is guided by a desire to help those who are suffering.

  • Transparency

    Flexibles Helps is committed to being transparent with its donors and partners in the allocation and management of its funds.

  • Equality

    Flexibles Helps is committed to respecting the rights of its beneficiaries and to treating everyone equally and with dignity.

  • Commitment

    Flexibles Helps puts its expertise, skills and energy at the service of its beneficiaries in a practical and sustainable way.

About Flexibles Helps

With over 06 years' experience at the heart of disaster-stricken areas, the members of Flexibles Helps have concrete expertise in humanitarian aid on the ground, which they have chosen to put at the service of local aid to help children and orphanages in specific areas and volunteers in East-West Europe and West Africa. With our in-depth knowledge of the local situation and its particularities, we are in the process of developing an organization on a human scale capable of carrying out targeted and sustainable actions for the most vulnerable. Flexibles Helps is a non-profit association under the French Law 290751. You can consult the complete statutes on request.

SOME KEY ASPECTS OF OUR WORK

The work of our NGO has a single objective: to respond to the real needs encountered in the field, which can be divided into categories of action.

The first : Like all orphanages, Flexible Help aims to bring up and educate destitute children or those from poor families, to protect them physically and morally from the dangers of poverty and idleness, and to enable them, when they are adults, to provide for themselves through work. To provide a place where children can grow and develop. This project involves building a new orphanage to take in children in difficult situations, particularly orphans and abandoned children.

The second : Emergency humanitarian aid. To this day, millions of civilians still fear for their survival, living without access to drinking water, without the ability to feed themselves, in a state of health and material insecurity, all year round, in every season... The aim of our work here is to address the urgency and seriousness of the situation in which they find themselves, and enable them to survive each day in the conditions they face.

The third : Development aid in poor towns in Europe and Africa. Convinced that the ultimate aim of humanitarian work is to bring about lasting improvements in living conditions, many of our actions in the field are part of this process of rebuilding many aspects of people's lives. Rehousing people in the camps, permanent access to education, building wells, support for the disabled and with it reintegration into social and professional life, (re)launching professional activities and returning to work, rebuilding in order to live again!
