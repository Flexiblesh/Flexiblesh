OUR VALUES AND COMMITMENTS

  • Solidarity

    Flexibles Helps's action is based on the responsibility to help the most disadvantaged and to support them in improving their daily lives.

  • Impartiality

    Flexibles Helps refuses any form of discrimination in its actions, and focuses solely on helping the most vulnerable.

  • Humanity

    Mobilized against human and animal suffering, which it considers intolerable, Flexibles Helps is guided by the desire to help those who suffer.

  • Transparency

    Flexibles Helps is committed to being transparent with its donors and partners in the allocation and management of its funds.

  • Equality

    Flexibles Helps is committed to respecting the rights of its beneficiaries, and to treating everyone equally and with dignity.

  • Engagement

    Flexibles Helps puts its expertise, skills and energy at the service of its beneficiaries in a concrete and sustainable way.
